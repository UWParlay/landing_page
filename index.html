What is Parlay?

Parlay Sports, founded in May 2019, is a digital sports betting service for American sports leagues. We are currently a free-to-play mobile app where sports fans can wager on upcoming games, compete against their friends, and improve over time with in app statistics. In the long term, Parlay will operate as a full fledged sports book unifying the United States recently legalized sports betting experience.
